About this dataset

Experimental data — not recommended for research use. This website is a prototype for testing data delivery and visualisation. Snowline and bare-ice extent values are experimental, have not been peer-reviewed, and should not be used in published research.

Copyright © Tom Chudley, University of Bristol.

Snowline elevations and bare-ice extents are derived from MODIS Terra MOD10A1 v6.1 daily snow-cover data (Hall & Riggs, 2021, NASA NSIDC DAAC), following an adapted method of Ryan et al. (2019, Science Advances). Basin and region boundaries follow Mouginot & Rignot (2019). Background map tiles © OpenStreetMap contributors, © CARTO.
